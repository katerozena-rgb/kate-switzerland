My Love for Switzerland

I visited Switzerland in the Spring of 2024 and absolutely loved it! The old-time feel, stunning landscapes, and amazing adventures made it an unforgettable experience. Here is a video that I think captures Switzerland's charm nicely.

My Highlights

Switzerland was so beautiful its hard to choose a favorite thing I did, so here are a couple of my highlights. Me and my sister paraglided over the town of Grindelwald which is something I would highly recommend to anyone seeking an adrenaline rush and breathtaking views. There are also these sheep that are just the cutest things ever, they are called Black Nose Sheep. We got to play and pet them and that was so much fun!

Things to Do in Switzerland

Switzerland offers plenty of activies for everybody in the family!

  1. Visit the Swiss Alps
    • Ski
    • Visit the Matterhorn
    • Ride a gondola for some awesome views
  2. Explore Swiss Cities
    • Visit old castles in Montreux
    • Go shopping in Geneva
    • Walk through the streets of Zurich
  3. Enjoy Swiss Cuisine
    • Try Swiss chocolate
    • Eat traditional fondue
    • Visit local markets for fresh produce
